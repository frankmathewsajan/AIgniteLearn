Welcome to Exploring Oceans & Atmosphere with PACE

This course is designed to take you on an exciting journey to explore the wonders of our oceans and atmosphere.

Through a combination of interactive lessons, engaging activities, and real-world examples, you'll gain a deeper understanding of the complex relationships between our planet's oceans and atmosphere.

Get Started!

Course Overview

  • Oceanography: Explore the world's oceans, including their formation, composition, and ecosystems.
  • Atmospheric Science: Learn about the Earth's atmosphere, including its structure, weather patterns, and climate systems.
  • PACE: Discover how human activities impact the oceans and atmosphere, and how we can work towards a more sustainable future.

What to Expect

  • Engage with interactive simulations and visualizations to explore complex concepts
  • Analyze real-world data and case studies to understand the impact of human activities on the oceans and atmosphere
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills through hands-on activities and discussions
  • Collaborate with peers to design and propose sustainable solutions to real-world problems

Course Outline

  • Module 1: Introduction to Oceanography
    • Overview of ocean formation and composition
    • Exploring ocean ecosystems and biodiversity
  • Module 2: Atmospheric Science Fundamentals
    • Structure and composition of the atmosphere
    • Weather patterns and climate systems
  • Module 3: Human Impact on the Oceans and Atmosphere
    • Understanding the effects of pollution, climate change, and overfishing
    • Exploring sustainable solutions and practices
  • Module 4: Designing a Sustainable Future
    • Collaborative project to design and propose sustainable solutions
    • Presenting and discussing project outcomes
