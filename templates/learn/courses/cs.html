Course Overview

The CS course is a professional qualification focusing on corporate governance and legal compliance. It consists of multiple exams including Foundation, Executive, and Professional stages.

Syllabus

  • Foundation Level: Basic knowledge of business laws, economics, and company accounts.
  • Executive Level: Advanced corporate laws, secretarial practices, and financial management.
  • Professional Level: In-depth governance, taxation, mergers & acquisitions, and compliance management.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as corporate secretaries, legal advisors, or governance specialists in both public and private sectors.

Salary

The average salary for a CS professional ranges from INR 5-8 lakhs per annum, depending on experience and expertise.
