Course Overview

The Pharm.D. is a professional doctorate focusing on pharmacy sciences, clinical practice, and patient care. It is designed to train pharmacists who can work in healthcare environments, advising on medications and treatment plans.

Syllabus

  • Year 1-2: Basic sciences, medicinal chemistry, pharmacy practice.
  • Year 3-4: Clinical pharmacy, therapeutic drug monitoring, patient counseling.
  • Year 5-6: Advanced clinical practice, hospital pharmacy, pharmacy management.

Career Opportunities

Pharm.D. graduates can work as clinical pharmacists, healthcare consultants, pharmacy educators, or in research and pharmaceutical companies.

Salary

Pharm.D. graduates can earn an average salary of INR 4-10 lakhs per annum depending on location, experience, and industry.
