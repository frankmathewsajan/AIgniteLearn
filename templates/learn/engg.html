Engineering Course: Module Overview

Welcome to the Engineering Course. Please choose one of the following options to begin.

1. Coding Exercise

Objective: Complete the coding exercises to practice and solidify your understanding of the engineering concepts.

Instructions:

  • Each exercise focuses on practical skills and applying theoretical knowledge.
  • Ensure to write clean and optimized code.
  • Use the provided IDE or text editor to submit your code.

Tasks:

  • Solve the problems in the given order.
  • Submit the completed code via the submission link.
  • You will receive instant feedback on your solutions.

2. Test

Objective: Assess your knowledge through a timed quiz.

Instructions:

  • The test consists of multiple-choice and short-answer questions.
  • You have 30 minutes to complete the test.
  • No external resources are allowed during the test.

Test Format:

  • Multiple-Choice Questions: Answer by selecting the correct option.
  • Short-Answer Questions: Type your answer in the provided space.

Grading: The test will be automatically graded once submitted. Results will be available immediately after submission.
